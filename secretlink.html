My fellow /vrg/ins, We are gathered here today to make VR great again with the first /vrg/ game jam. The "/vrg/ Party" game jam!! Don't have the motivation to create a vr game from scratch? Lack the skills in blender? Don't even knowwhere to begin? THEN START HERE!

A mini-game is a small, yet easy to create, game thats easy to explain in a short time and doesn't take long to finsh playing. Just like those Mario Party mini games! Luckly we already have a platform for "easy" multiplayer and scence viewing with VRChat. Although not everyones choice, it does offer benifites with low stakes multiplayer mini games. So if something bugs out, its not that big of a deal. And kicking shit around is always fun with friends.

Here you can figure out some skills for game deving. With recent events proving that we need better, stronger mini-games. This is the perfect optertunitiy to contribute to the greater VR good. Your mini-game will be available to those making worlds via downloadable links on the webpage. That way no event is left with out some thing to do on the side.

RULES:

Mini Game must be explainable in Video/Point Cloud Video in less than 1.5 minute

Mini Game must be made for VR usage

Mini Game must state if Full Body Tracking is needed

Mini Game must have a fixed time limit. Max 5 mins.

Mini Game filesize packed should* be less than 50MB *(some leway allowed)

Mini Game must be postable on blue boards(no NSFW content)

Mini Game must be made for VRChat(boo) using MUGI

Mini Game must be multiplayer 2 to 8 players

Mini Game can be made in teams or solo

Mini Game must have a clear winner(s)/loser(s)/ties

Mini Game must be follow the template constrains (dimensions, logo, ect). More info in the doc.

"off the shelf" assest allowed. Must be credited/stated. More info in the doc.

AI Slop allowed, but not recommended for everything

Submission(s) must include VRChat username(s) of all who contrubited to the Mini Game. More info in the doc.

Multiple submissions allowed
